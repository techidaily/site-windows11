## Navigating Language Diversity: Windows Font Download

 If you frequently work with different languages, you might want to ensure you can get every font available for them. And while you can install fonts individually as needed, it can be inconvenient, especially if you're in the middle of work.

 So, why not download every available font for all languages in Windows? Remember, though, that doing so will take up a lot of your storage, so ensure you have enough free space to accommodate them.

 Check out the steps below to see how you can download fonts for all languages in the Settings app and the Control Panel. Let's get to it.

## How to Download Fonts for All Languages in Settings

 The easiest way to download every language font on Windows is to use the Settings app. The steps should be similar whether you're using Windows 10 or 11\. Here's how:

1. Press**Win + I** to open the Settings app and head to**Personalization > Fonts** .
2. In the**Related settings** section, click on**Download fonts for all languages** . On Windows 11, you'll have to expand the section first; on Windows 10, you'll have to scroll down, past the fonts, to find it.  
![Windows 11 Fonts screen in the Settings app with the Related settings section showing](https://static1.makeuseofimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/windows-11-personalization-fonts-related-settings.jpg)
3. In the pop-up, click**OK** .  
![a pop up asking if you want to continue downloading fonts for all languages on Windows](https://static1.makeuseofimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/popup-donwnload-all-fonts-windows-11.jpg)

 Depending on your internet speed, the time it takes to download all fonts will vary. If your internet is slow, check out[how to increase your internet speed on Windows 11](https://www.makeuseof.com/windows-11-increase-internet-speed/) .

## How to Download Fonts for All Languages in the Control Panel

 You can also download fonts for all languages in the Control Panel. So,[open the Control Panel,](https://www.makeuseof.com/windows-open-control-panel/) then follow the steps below.

1. In the Control Panel, head to**Appearance and Personalization > Fonts** .
2. In the left panel, click on**Download fonts for all languages** .  
![the Fonts screen in the Control Panel on Windows](https://static1.makeuseofimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/fonts-control-panel-windows.jpg)
3. In the pop-up, click**OK** .
