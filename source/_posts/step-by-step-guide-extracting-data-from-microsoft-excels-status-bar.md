##  Copy the Status Bar Values

 Once you select the cells and see the calculations at the bottom, simply click the value you want in the Status Bar. This places it on your clipboard. You can then [paste](https://facebook-videos.techidaily.com/new-ultimate-collection-20-chuckle-inducing-detention-cell-photos-for-a-better-day-for-2024/) it directly in your sheet or another application.

Related: [How to Paste Text Without Formatting Almost Anywhere](https://facebook-videos.techidaily.com/new-ultimate-collection-20-chuckle-inducing-detention-cell-photos-for-a-better-day-for-2024/) 

 To paste it into your spreadsheet, select the cell where you want the value and then do one of the following:

* [Right-click](https://desktop-recording.techidaily.com/updated-the-art-of-recording-fun-6-techniques-to-document-minecraft-for-2024/) and choose "Paste."
* Go to the Home tab and click "Paste" in the Clipboard section of the ribbon.
* Use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+V.
